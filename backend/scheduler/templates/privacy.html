Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2025

Welcome to Course Schedule Manager (the “Service,” “Web App,” or “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services, including integrations with third-party providers such as Google Calendar.

By using Course Schedule Manager, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the Service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information
    When you authorize our integration with Google Calendar, we may access limited profile information (e.g., your Google email address) and data needed to schedule your courses/events.
  • Calendar Events
    Upon user consent, we may insert, update, or retrieve events from your Google Calendar to provide core features (e.g., adding course schedules).
  • Non-Personal Information
    We may collect technical data such as browser type, operating system, and pages visited. This data is used for analytics and service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and Maintain the Service
    We use your Google Calendar permissions solely to create or modify events in your calendar, as requested by you.
  • Improve User Experience
    We analyze usage data and incorporate feedback to refine features and enhance performance.
  • Customer Support
    If you contact us, we may use your provided information to respond and resolve any issues.

3. Google User Data

When you connect your Google account to Course Schedule Manager, you grant specific permission scopes (e.g., Google Calendar). We do not store your Google password or other sensitive credentials. We will not sell or share your Google user data with third parties for marketing purposes. Access to and use of Google data will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • Cookies
    We may use cookies to remember user preferences, authenticate sessions, and personalize your experience.
  • Analytics
    We may use third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to collect anonymous usage data, helping us understand and improve our Service.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Service Providers
    We may share limited data with trusted third-party vendors (e.g., hosting providers or analytics services) who help us run the Service. They are required to not use your data for other purposes.
  • Legal Requirements
    We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by government authorities.
  • Business Transfers
    If Course Schedule Manager is involved in a merger or acquisition, user information may be transferred. We will notify you before your data is transferred under a different privacy policy.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no security measure is 100% perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. After this period, we securely delete or anonymize the data.

8. Children's Privacy

Course Schedule Manager is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information promptly.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Correction: You can contact us to request access to or correction of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Consent Withdrawal: You may revoke Google permissions at any time via your Google Account settings or by contacting us directly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last Updated” date at the top will be revised. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically for any updates or changes.
